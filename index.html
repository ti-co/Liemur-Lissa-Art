Born and raised in moscow - educated at Moscow State University. I'm a citizen of the world with close ties to Asia, Europe and Central America. Thailand is my paradise. I take life on earth as a gift.

My paintings express my gratitude to this wonderful life. I find inspiration in natural elements: light, shadows, reflections and the movement of the Ocean. You will see the impressions of my travels reflected in my paintings and video footage.
